ABOUT THE SORBET

Embark on a journey of vibrant flavors with our Lemon Basil Sorbet paired with Prosecco Gelée—a refreshing dessert duo that perfectly complements the bright and zesty flavors of our Grilled Lemon Basil Chicken. Imagine a scoop of velvety Lemon Basil Sorbet, bursting with the tangy essence of freshly squeezed lemons and the aromatic herbaceousness of basil. Each spoonful is a burst of sunshine, awakening the palate with its refreshing citrusy notes and delicate herbal undertones. But what truly elevates this dessert duo is the addition of Prosecco Gelée—a delicate gelatin dessert infused with the effervescent sparkle of Prosecco wine.

The Prosecco Gelée adds a touch of elegance and sophistication to the dessert, with its light and airy texture and subtle sweetness balancing the tartness of the sorbet. Together, the Lemon Basil Sorbet and Prosecco Gelée create a symphony of flavors and textures that dance on the palate. The bright acidity of the lemon sorbet is tempered by the sweetness of the Prosecco Gelée, creating a harmonious balance that refreshes and delights with each spoonful. Pair our Lemon Basil Sorbet with Prosecco Gelée with our Grilled Lemon Basil Chicken for a dining experience that is both refreshing and satisfying. The bright and zesty flavors of the sorbet complement the herbaceousness of the chicken perfectly, creating a harmony of flavors that will leave you craving more.
