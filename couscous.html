ABOUT THE COUSCOUS

Transport your taste buds to the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean with our Herbed Couscous Pilaf—a flavorful side dish that perfectly complements a variety of main courses, such as our succulent Grilled Mediterranean Chicken with Roasted Vegetables. Imagine fluffy pearls of couscous, delicately seasoned with a blend of aromatic herbs and spices, including parsley, mint, and oregano. Each grain is infused with the essence of the Mediterranean, providing a burst of fresh flavor with every forkful. But what truly sets our Herbed Couscous Pilaf apart is the addition of a colorful array of vegetables—diced bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and zucchini—sauteed to perfection and mixed into the couscous. These vibrant vegetables not only add a pop of color to the dish but also contribute a variety of textures and flavors, creating a symphony of tastes that dance on your palate.

As you take a bite of our Herbed Couscous Pilaf, you'll be greeted by a harmonious blend of flavors—the nuttiness of the couscous, the freshness of the herbs, and the sweetness of the vegetables—all coming together to create a culinary masterpiece that is both satisfying and nutritious. Pair our Herbed Couscous Pilaf with our Grilled Mediterranean Chicken for a meal that transports you to the shores of Greece or Italy with each mouthful. The lightness of the couscous pilaf complements the bold flavors of the chicken perfectly, creating a balanced and satisfying dining experience that will leave you longing for more.
