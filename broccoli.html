ABOUT THE BROCCOLI

Dive into a culinary experience that celebrates simplicity and elegance with our Steamed Broccoli with Homemade Hollandaise Sauce—a timeless side dish that perfectly complements a variety of main courses, such as our succulent Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill Butter. Imagine tender broccoli florets, gently steamed to perfection to retain their vibrant color and crisp texture. Each bite offers a burst of freshness and wholesome goodness, providing a refreshing counterpart to the richness of the hollandaise sauce. But what truly elevates this dish is the addition of homemade hollandaise sauce—a velvety, buttery emulsion with a rich and decadent flavor that coats each broccoli floret in a luxurious embrace. Made with clarified butter, egg yolks, and a splash of lemon juice, our hollandaise sauce strikes the perfect balance between indulgence and refinement, adding depth and complexity to the dish.

As you indulge in a forkful of our Steamed Broccoli with Homemade Hollandaise Sauce, you'll experience a symphony of flavors and textures. The crisp freshness of the broccoli contrasts beautifully with the silky richness of the hollandaise sauce, creating a harmonious balance that delights the palate and leaves you craving more. Pair our Steamed Broccoli with Homemade Hollandaise Sauce with our Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill Butter for a culinary experience that is both satisfying and indulgent. The lightness of the broccoli complements the richness of the salmon perfectly, creating a symphony of flavors that will transport you to culinary heaven with each mouthful.
