ABOUT THE TIRAMISU

Experience a harmonious blend of Eastern and Western culinary traditions with our Matcha Green Tea Tiramisu—a unique dessert that perfectly complements a variety of main courses, such as our delicate Sakura Salmon with Wasabi Aioli. Imagine layers of delicate ladyfinger biscuits, soaked in a fragrant matcha green tea syrup that infuses each bite with the earthy essence of green tea. These tender biscuits form the foundation of our tiramisu, providing a light and airy texture that melts in your mouth with each spoonful. But what truly sets our Matcha Green Tea Tiramisu apart is the luscious mascarpone cream filling, delicately flavored with matcha green tea powder and sweetened with a hint of honey.

This creamy concoction adds a decadent richness to the dessert, balancing the subtle bitterness of the matcha with a creamy sweetness that tantalizes the taste buds. As you take a spoonful of our Matcha Green Tea Tiramisu, you'll experience a symphony of flavors—the bold earthiness of the matcha, the creamy sweetness of the mascarpone, and the delicate crunch of the ladyfingers—all coming together in perfect harmony to create a dessert that is both indulgent and refreshing. Pair our Matcha Green Tea Tiramisu with our Sakura Salmon with Wasabi Aioli for a culinary experience that is truly unforgettable. The lightness of the tiramisu complements the richness of the salmon perfectly, creating a balance of flavors that will leave you craving more with each bite.
