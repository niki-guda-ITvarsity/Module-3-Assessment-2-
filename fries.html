ABOUT THE FRIES

Indulge your senses with our Truffle Parmesan Fries—a luxurious side dish that perfectly complements a variety of main courses, such as our succulent Wagyu Beef Sliders with Gourmet Toppings. Imagine golden, crispy fries, perfectly cooked to achieve a delightful crunch on the outside while remaining fluffy and tender on the inside. Each fry is generously coated in a tantalizing blend of truffle oil and grated Parmesan cheese, infusing it with an irresistible aroma and rich, savory flavor that will leave you craving more. As you bite into a fry, you're greeted by the unmistakable earthy aroma of black truffles, mingling with the nutty richness of the Parmesan cheese. The delicate crunch gives way to a velvety interior, releasing a burst of umami goodness that dances on your palate with each bite.

But what truly sets our Truffle Parmesan Fries apart is the finishing touch—a sprinkle of freshly chopped parsley that adds a vibrant pop of color and a hint of freshness to the dish. This simple garnish not only enhances the visual appeal of the fries but also provides a subtle herbal note that balances the richness of the truffle and Parmesan flavors. Pair our Truffle Parmesan Fries with our Wagyu Beef Sliders for a culinary experience that is truly unforgettable. The indulgent richness of the fries complements the savory perfection of the sliders, creating a symphony of flavors and textures that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you longing for more.
