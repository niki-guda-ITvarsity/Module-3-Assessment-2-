ABOUT THE SEA BASS

Immerse yourself in a culinary journey of freshness and vibrancy with our Grilled Sea Bass with Citrus Salsa—a tantalizing seafood masterpiece that celebrates the essence of spring and summer on your plate. At the heart of this dish lies a generous portion of tender and flaky sea bass, grilled to perfection to impart a delicate smokiness that enhances the natural sweetness of the fish. Each succulent fillet is expertly seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices, infusing it with layers of flavor that dance on your palate with every bite.

Accompanying our grilled sea bass is a vibrant Citrus Salsa, bursting with the bright flavors of ripe oranges, tangy grapefruits, and zesty lemons. Diced red onions, jalapeños, and fresh cilantro add a kick of heat and herbal freshness, while a drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil brings a silky richness that ties the salsa together harmoniously. To complete this culinary symphony, we serve our Grilled Sea Bass atop a bed of herb-infused quinoa, cooked to perfection with a fragrant blend of fresh herbs such as parsley, dill, and basil. This wholesome and nutritious grain provides a delightful contrast of textures and flavors, complementing the lightness of the sea bass and enhancing the overall dining experience.
