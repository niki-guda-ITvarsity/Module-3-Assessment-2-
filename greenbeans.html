ABOUT THE GREENBEANS

Elevate your dining experience with our Sautéed Green Beans with Almonds—a delightful side dish that perfectly complements a variety of main courses, including our succulent Grilled Sea Bass with Citrus Salsa. Imagine crisp and tender green beans, delicately sautéed to perfection in a fragrant blend of garlic-infused olive oil and butter. Each bite offers a burst of freshness and vibrant green color, capturing the essence of farm-fresh produce at its peak. But what truly sets our Sautéed Green Beans apart is the addition of toasted almonds—a crunchy and aromatic garnish that adds a delightful nutty flavor and satisfying crunch to every forkful

Toasted to golden perfection, these almonds provide a textural contrast that elevates the dish to new heights of culinary excellence. To finish, a sprinkle of freshly grated lemon zest adds a burst of citrusy brightness that cuts through the richness of the butter and adds a refreshing twist to the dish. The lemon zest not only enhances the flavor of the green beans but also provides a visually stunning garnish that adds a pop of color to the plate. Pair our Sautéed Green Beans with Almonds with our Grilled Sea Bass with Citrus Salsa for a culinary experience that is truly unforgettable
